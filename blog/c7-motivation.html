Life looks good. When reading this I kept relating it to getting a high. Things like drugs or alcohol providing a temporary pleasure. Getting a Passion high would be like completing a gym exercise and almost forgetting you have done it yet you’re getting the benefits. Sitting there thinking that nothing can take the high away of raising someone who has reached such a high themselves. A full sense of purpose in life.

Explaining the failure and moving on.

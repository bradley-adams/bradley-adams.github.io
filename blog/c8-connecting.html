Complete the political awareness exercise here in writing.

My situation would be borrowing money from family. Someone I know borrowed some money from their father for a car. They organised the loan with interest, repayments, etc. The other siblings got wind of the situation and didn't see it as a business deal rather than ripping the father off. The son who bought the car thought he did the right thing setting it up as a traditional loan but didn’t inform his siblings. His sisters did jump to conclusions but should have ask the details before speculating. The son wants a car for his family in the right way but the sisters don’t want their father taken advantage off. Both are reasonable intentions.

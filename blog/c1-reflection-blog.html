B W Adams Video Reflection

21st November 2017

Whats your take on the DBC/EDA experience? I believe it is different from any other learning enviroment I have come across in the past. Genuinely colaborate. Very involved and fast paced.

What are your impressions? It feels good so far. I get a great feeling of achievement from the learning. Its a process of change which I find hard and rewarding.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture? A little unsure. I haven't had any expereince in this industry nor do I know anyone who works in it. I like meeting new people and engaging with them which should help. I think being genuine and staying involved will be key.

Have your expectiations of EDA changed? If so, how? Yes. I came into this expecting my skills to change. Now I am expecting to have a new outlook on a lot of topics including different cultures, how I'm learning and how I understand people.

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning enviroment? Does it make you nervous? Yes at the same time. I was very nervous and still am. But the more I do the more I find it turning into excitment. My confidence is growing with the parts I complete.

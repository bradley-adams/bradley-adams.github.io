What learnings did you take from the chapter?

The best bit that I took away was that in finding out where you are emotionally makes it easier to find out where others are at. You need emotional intelligence for others to almost unlock your own general intelligence. In gaining emotional intelligence you can almost improve what your already doing. It can even create new opportunities like leadership. The final learning that stood out would be around Body and Mind. With trying to find your own emotions it is sometimes easier and quicker to listen to your body rather than your mind.

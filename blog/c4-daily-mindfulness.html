Daily Mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I tried it with my wife and found it a little hard. I found that I already dabble in looping but I always figured it was because I have terrible hearing. I did find it easiest to put away the phones and turn off the TV then try to do this over dinner. It game us time for pauses and didn't make it as awkward if no one was saying anything. I felt better after dinner. Like we had achieved something while conversing. I learn't that what I thought she was talking about vs what she was trying to say often took 2 or 3 more loops to get to the same conclusion. This surprised me as we have lived together for 5 years or more and if there is anyone I would want to understand fully it would be her. She did find it awkward but I think enjoyed it in the end. I think she got to talk about a lot of organizing things which helped her a lot. Especially because I couldn't interrupt.

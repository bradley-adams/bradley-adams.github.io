What is an analogy to Desibe HTML, CSS and Dom?

I would probably use the building analogy from the Udacity videos. A house needs structure which is similar to a website needs HTML. CSS is the design like flooring, painting and furniture. Dom can be described as a access or visiting the hoouse. Someone used a key or remote to get in or acces the house and interprets what you have built and arranged

What is boxifying design?

In simple it means every website is made up on boxes and in most cases boxes inside boxes. Even if things appear to be circles they are boxes in terms of space allocated to them on the page.

What is the box model?

Esentially it helps you determin the widths and heights of an element. For example you can have a picture with and ousite that thing like padding, border and margins add to it. So your actual width of you elemnt is content plus those other items.
